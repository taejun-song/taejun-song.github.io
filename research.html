Research Interests

My research focuses on problems where mathematical structure directly informs modeling decisions, system design, and evaluation rigor.

Current Focus

  • Genome editing outcome prediction using sequence- and representation-based models
  • Protein modeling and large-scale structure prediction pipelines
  • Representation learning with strong inductive bias and biological constraints
  • Reliable ML systems: evaluation rigor, data leakage prevention, reproducibility
  • Agentic and retrieval-augmented systems for scientific research assistance

Approach

I believe strong mathematical intuition is the most transferable skill across research, engineering, and quantitative disciplines. My work emphasizes:

  • Rigorous evaluation methodologies
  • Reproducible experimental design
  • Principled model architecture decisions
  • Scalable system engineering
